Simon的雅思翻转课堂

重音的阅读方式为 下上下，需要重读的场景：

  1. 主谓宾的宾语
  2. 短语的最后一个
  3. 你所表达的关键词语
    一般重读都是名词。

练习积累

练习了part1, Are you interested in fashion or clothes fashion?
答：No, I’m not interested in fashion. Rather than fashion, I focus more on the functionality of clothes. When I climb mountains, I will choose outdoor jacket although it may make me look bad.

常用搭配积累：
keep warm
look bad
outdoor jacket
